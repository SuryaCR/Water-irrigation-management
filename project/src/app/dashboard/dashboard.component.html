Water Management

Water management is the control and movement of water resources to minimize damage to life and property and to maximize efficient beneficial use. Good water management of dams and levees reduces the risk of harm due to flooding.



Water Irrigation Level

Irrigation water has an RSC 8.5 meq l−1 and it needs to be reduced to 2.5 meq l−1. The water required for irrigation is 800 mm per hectare for the complete growing period of the sorghum crop.
